Who I am

I'm not just advising on visas.
I've lived one.

My name is Dean Patrick Weischede — Pat. I'm 26, and I've spent the last few years turning my own immigration story into a career that means something. I came to New Zealand from the Philippines as a kid, watched my family navigate a system that doesn't forgive mistakes, and decided that was exactly the kind of work I wanted to do.

Today I'm a fully licensed immigration adviser (IAA #202300030) at NZ Immigration Help Service Ltd — the family business my mother founded. I work across Auckland and Manila, helping individuals and employers navigate New Zealand's immigration system with clarity and confidence.

Outside the office, I'm deep in AI automation, building systems that make immigration advisory more efficient, more scalable, and more human. And on weekends, I run. Badly, then less badly, then sometimes well.
